Sheeps' Milk Cheese

PAVÉ COBBLE

INFORMATION

Moulded into a truncated pyramid, the paste is springy and soft, becoming meltingly creamy with age.
With a light coating of ash, the geotrichum and glaucum moulds grow on the rind developing silvery grey rind at first, then becoming dominated by blues and greens.
On first taste, notes of sweet cream emerge, followed by nutty flavours and a slight lanolin gaminess that deepens the complexity with the age of the cheese.
The cheese is named Pavé Cobble by Roger (Director) a keen cyclist, as a homage to the difficult and
famous cobbled cycling stages.
Supreme Champion of the British Cheese Awards 2017.
Made with unpasteurised milk and vegetarian rennet.

LITTLE SHE

INFORMATION

This is a soft white cheese with a bloomy rind. Made in the style of a brie or Camembert this cheese will soften as it ripens. This cheese has a mild flavour becoming more pronounced and the rind can become more colourful as it ages.
Made using unpasteurised milk and vegetarian rennet

SHEEP RUSTLER

INFORMATION

A Ewes milk version of our well known Rachel cheese.
This is a Semi hard Cheese aged to around 3 months old.
This cheese has a colourful orange/brown rind.
This cheese has a medium, mellow, nutty flavour
with hints of caramel.
Supreme Champion of the British Cheese Awards 2018.
Made with unpasteurised milk and vegetarian rennet
