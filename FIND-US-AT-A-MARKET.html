FIND US AT A MARKET


The reason why we love making cheese at White Lake comes down to all you cheeselovers out there. We can regularly be found at a variety of markets in the UK, chatting cheese and listening to our customers thoughts. If you would like to come meet the guys to sample some cheese, or have any ideas for what the next White Lake masterpiece could be, please come pay us a visit!

Please find below the regular markets that we attend. We can also be found at other food events through the year such as the amazing Eat Festivals, Abergavenny Food Festival, The Royal Bath and West Show to name a few. Please follow our social media accounts for updates on where you can find us.
